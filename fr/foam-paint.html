DIY Sidewalk Fizzy Foam Paint

A fun, cheap, and easy DIY to make that will keep the kids busy playing outside!

What do you need:

  • 1 box baking soda
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • About 1 1/2 cups of shaving foam
  • Food coloring drops
  • Squirt bottles
  • White vinegar
  • Spray bottle

How do you play it:

  1. Pour water into baking soda and mix them together.
  2. Add the shaving foam to the combination.
  3. Transfer the fizzy foam paint into five small cups or dishes.
  4. Add 7 drops of your desired food coloring to each cup.
  5. Transfer the paint to the squirt bottle.
  6. Pour white vinegar into a spray bottle.
  7. Start drawing and spray the vinegar on it.

You will have about 5 bottles that are about 1/2 cup each at the end.

Tips:

If the fizzy foam paint seems too thick or unpourable, add a little bit of water. And the more vinegar it is sprayed the more it fluffy.

Note:

You can clean the fizzy foam paint with the squirt of a hose or a pitcher of water. If you do not clean it after a day or so, it will turn into a white powdery substance and will be washed away with the hose or rain with no staining the driveway or sidewalk.
