Indoor Educational

Here are some indoor educational activities to keep the kids busy and educated.

Building a Bridge with Straws

Build a bridge out of straws and straight pins that can support the weight of 100 pennies in a small cup. Building a strong and stable structure requires an understanding of the forces that will act on the structure under loads of various kinds. Read More

DIY Catapult for Kids

This is a STEM project to build a catapult with given supplies without instructions. The kids get to be engineers and truly have to think in order to complete the given task. Read More

5 Coolest Things With Coke

Making Chemistry reactions with Coca-cola and other materials to create observable phenomena. This experiment introduces the basic knowledge of chemistry, understanding of the changes of the material like colors and phases etc. Read More

Salty Ice

‘Salty Ice’ is a great science activity for kids that will teach them how the freezing point of water can be lowered with salt! Kids will learn about the physical properties of water by the end of the fun science activity. Read More
