Playdough Maze with Straw

What you will need:

  • Playdough
  • Ping pong ball or any light weight ball
  • Straw(s)

How do you make it:

  1. Roll Playdough into long lines.
  2. Use the long Playdough line to make a maze. You could make it harder if the path of the maze is narrower.
  3. Lastly, use the straws to blow the lightweight ball from the start of the course to the end. If the ball passes the path, you will have to restart.

Tips:

There are different variations of this game:

  • For younger kids, it might be too hard to blow the ping pong ball. You could make a small paper ball for them to blow instead.
  • You don't need to use the straw, you can just blow the light weight ball directly.
  • You could also make this in a cardboard box and move the box to make the ball travel through the maze instead.
  • Others have also added straws as an element of the maze.

Note:

What do kids learn from the Playdough maze? First they could use their imagination to design the maze. Also, they could practice the control to move the ball through the maze. More importantly, it is a very fun game to play with friends and family.
